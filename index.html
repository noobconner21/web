RATH NAPURA

The City of Gems

"The City of Gems", Ratnapura is famous for its long history of gem mining and trading. For centuries, Ratnapura has been a major source of precious stones including rubies, sapphires, and cat's eyes. These gems can be found in the valleys around the town, and are often prepared by Muslim gem cutters. You can see examples of these beautiful stones on display at the Ratnapura Gem Museum.Besides gems, Ratnapura is also known for its production of rice, fruits, tea, and rubber. Large plantations surround the city, producing both low-country tea and rubber.


Ratnapura is a great place to visit if you're interested in Sri Lankan culture, history, and natural beauty. The city is a major crossroads between the southern plains and the hill country to the east.
